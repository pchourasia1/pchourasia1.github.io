About Me

I am Prakash Chourasia, Graduate student at Department of Computer Science Georgia State University Atlanta GA. I am a Ph.D. candidate advised by Dr. Murray Patterson at the Department of Computer Science at Georgia State University. I am an aspiring Research Professional, well equipped with practical and innovative solutions. I am a staunch believer that diligence and determination with a focused goal are necessary for the long run. My research interest are in the fields of Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Federated Learning, Representation learning, Kernel methods, Algorithms, Bioinformatics, Drug Discovery, and Health data. My current research focuses on developing ML/AI techniques in the field of bioinformatics.

Previously, I earned my Masters Degree in Computer Science from Georgia State University in 2014-16.

I have 5+ years of experience in Software/IT Industry. I worked at Amerisave Mortgage Corporation. and Incomm as a Software Engineer for 5 years. I received my Bachelors of Engineering in Information Technology from Rajiv Gandhi Technical University, Bhopal, India.
